## **Welcome to Roma Mart’s Technology Hub**

Roma Mart is a family-run convenience store committed to bringing innovation to the heart of our community.
Here, we explore new ways to use technology—data, analytics, and smart software—to serve our customers better and modernize our operations.

---

### **What We Do**

* Develop internal analytics and automation tools
* Experiment with computer vision and AI for store insights
* Build modern, customer-facing web applications
* Document our processes, tech stack, and vision for future growth

---

### **Why We Do It**

* To empower Roma Mart to make smarter business decisions
* To improve customer experience and efficiency
* To keep our team learning, growing, and building for the future

---

## **About This Organization**

This GitHub organization is the central space for all of Roma Mart’s tech projects and documentation.

**Most of our code and tools are private to protect our customers and our business,
but we believe in transparency and learning—so you’ll find some public documentation, roadmaps, and inspiration here!**
